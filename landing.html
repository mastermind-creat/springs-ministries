About Springs Ministries

Founded in 2010, Springs Ministries has been a beacon of hope for vulnerable children and widows in underserved communities.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to impacting thousands of lives

Our Journey

Springs Ministries was born out of a deep conviction to address the plight of orphans and widows who often face systemic neglect and marginalization. What started as a small initiative has grown into a movement transforming communities.

How It Started

In 2010, our founder, Sarah Johnson, encountered a group of orphaned children living on the streets during a mission trip. Touched by their plight, she started by providing meals for 10 children twice a week from her own kitchen. This small act of compassion planted the seed for what would become Springs Ministries.

Within a year, with the help of volunteers, we established our first children's home, providing shelter, education, and family-like care for 15 orphans. The ministry was officially registered as a non-profit organization in 2011.

Our Growth

From helping 10 children in 2010 to over 500 annually today

Our Team

What We Do

Education Programs

We provide quality education through scholarships, school supplies, tutoring programs, and our own learning centers. Our holistic approach ensures children receive both academic instruction and life skills training.

Shelter & Care

Our children's homes provide a safe, family-like environment for orphans. We also run foster care programs and work to reunite children with extended family when possible, providing support to ensure their wellbeing.

Economic Empowerment

We equip widows and vulnerable women with vocational skills, provide microloans for small businesses, and offer financial literacy training to break the cycle of poverty and create sustainable livelihoods.
